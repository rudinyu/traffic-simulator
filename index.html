What to Watch

  • Red road segments show congestion, while yellow segments show lower speeds.
  • Seeded incidents appear at random times and clear automatically after 30–120 simulated minutes.
  • Bus priority extends the green phase when a bus approaches the intersection.
  • Highway braking delay combines reaction time with brake build-up time before deceleration begins.
  • Wet and icy roads reduce friction, lengthen stopping distance, and increase following gaps.
